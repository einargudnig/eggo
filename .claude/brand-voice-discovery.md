## 5. Brand personality

From `.impeccable.md`: **Capable, warm, direct.** A skilled craftsperson who explains things plainly and delivers reliably. No jargon, no posturing — confidence backed by competence.

## 6. Voice traits (extracted from live copy)

1. **Direct, no padding.** _"Ekkert bull, ekkert óþarfi."_
2. **Client-respecting, ownership-first.** _"Þú átt vöruna. Við byggjum hana."_ / _"Fullt eignarhald."_
3. **Humanist about tech.** _"Tæknin á að þjóna fólki. Ekki öfugt."_
4. **Culturally grounded.** Manifesto built on the phrase _"Þetta reddast"_ — used playfully, then subverted into "we'll actually sort this out properly."
5. **Pragmatic encouragement.** _"Lítið er betra en ekki neitt."_ / _"Það má vera með litla krúttlega heimasíðu."_

## 7. Rhythm & mechanics

- Short sentences. Often a two-part structure joined by an em-dash.
- Italics used sparingly on one or two key words (`<em>sem virkar.</em>`).
- Pronoun: _við_ (we) — first-person plural, collaborative.
- Almost no jargon. AI is _gervigreind_, not "AI."

## 8. Visual system (already defined)

- **Accent color:** Warm amber `#d4a054` (dark) / `#b8863a` (light). Deliberately NOT emerald green (avoided as the "generic consultancy default").
- **Neutrals:** Stone-tinted, warm.
- **Type:** Instrument Serif (headings) + DM Sans (body).
- **Modes:** Dark default, both themes polished.
- **Design principle:** _"Let the words work"_ — design amplifies copy, doesn't compete with it.

## 9. Conflicts / tensions

1. **Two voices on one site.** Homepage is plain and pragmatic; manifesto is philosophical and poetic. Both work, but advertising requires a decision about which surface applies where.
2. **Bluntness vs warmth.** _"Ekkert bull"_ (blunt) and the "warm craftsperson" positioning can coexist, but the ratio needs a rule.
